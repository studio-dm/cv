About

  • 2012-2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    StudioDM was founded by David Morgan. While working as a software engineer, he made a mission to find best practices and put emphasis on Responsive design. His vision is for all to connect and make a better web.

  • February 2016

    Mary Overfield Joins David Morgan

    It was at a coffee shop that David met Mary. Laptop to Laptop they had an idea for a designers blog which later came to fruition as the "The Web Designers Journal". Mary has been a major contributor to various blogs and social media marketing.
